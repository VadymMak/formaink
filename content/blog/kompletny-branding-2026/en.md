## What does a complete branding package include?

A complete branding package is a set of visual materials designed at once, in one style, by one designer. The basic package for a restaurant or small business includes a menu or product flyer, business cards (100 pcs with print), one advertising banner or poster, and a social media kit. Package price is **€350–€550** — ordering everything together saves 15–20% compared to individual orders.

The extended package — Visual Identity Basic at **€1,500–€3,500** — adds a logo, color palette, typography, brand guide PDF and social media templates. This package is ideal for businesses starting out or rebranding.

## Why is consistent visual identity more important than ever?

Your customer sees your business in three places simultaneously: on Instagram, on your business card, and at your venue or menu. If each of these looks different, the brain doesn't connect them to one brand. Nielsen Norman Group research shows that consistent visual identity increases brand recognition by 80% compared to inconsistent materials.

For Adriano Restaurant in Trenčín, we delivered a complete brandbook including menu guidelines in 2025. The result: production time for each new print material dropped from 5 days to 2 days, because every new designer or printer had precise rules for what to do and how.

## When is a brandbook worth ordering instead of just a logo?

A brandbook is worth it when you have more than one type of print material, more than one supplier, or plan to grow. A brandbook for **€450–€1,000** includes a color palette with CMYK and RGB values, typographic hierarchy, logo usage rules on light and dark backgrounds, and application examples on business cards, menus and social media. Without a brandbook, every new order from a different printer or designer risks deviation from the original design.

For Adriano, we delivered a 15-page brandbook for **€450–€600** in 3 weeks. The restaurant uses it for both locations — in Trenčín and Vienna.

It suits restaurants, cafés and businesses with seasonal offerings that change their menu or prices every 3 months. ## What happens if I keep ordering design piece by piece?

Ordering graphics piece by piece has three concrete risks. **First: time loss** — every new order from a new designer means re-explaining your style, colours and fonts. **Second: higher cost** — individual orders cost 15–20% more than a package. **Third: inconsistency** — different designers will deliver different shades of the same colour, different logo sizes, different fonts. After 2–3 years you have materials that don't connect with each other.
